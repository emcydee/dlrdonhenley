About Me

Hello, my name is Don and I am a Game Developer and Graphic Designer based in the Philippines. I love bringing creativity and tech together to create awesome games and eye-catching designs. Join me on this exciting journey where imagination meets technology!

So, Who Am I?


I completed my studies at LPU-Batangas, earning a degree in BS Computer Science with a major in Game Development. I have a passion for playing games, and creating them brings me immense joy. I've gained extensive experience in game development, particularly using Unity and Buildbox, and when coding, I typically use the C# language. Additionally, I enjoy graphic design, whether it's for my game projects or other purposes. Transforming creative ideas into actual games is not only enjoyable but also a fulfilling process for me, thanks to my creative and detail-oriented approach. I combine my artistic skills with technical know-how to craft engaging gaming experiences.


Check out my portfolio to see the different projects that showcase my skills and unique creative perspective. If you have any questions or if you want to work on a project together, feel free to contact me!

Blob

Embark on an exhilarating arcade journey for kids aged 8 and up, collecting crystals and dodging obstacles with a simple tap. Navigate chasms, evade enemies, and unlock new game types and characters for endless hours of fun and adventure!

Gloo

Navigate vibrant levels, enhance cognitive skills, and have fun with easy controls and playful graphics. Kid-ventures is the ideal game for your child's growth and learning!
